SeeSpan

Identifies U.S. members of Congress in C-SPAN program videos and overlays their top campaign contributors and recent stock trades for whoever is speaking.

No setup required — there are no API keys to configure. The extension reads pre-built, publicly-sourced data files (campaign contributions from the FEC; trades from official House/Senate financial disclosures) and refreshes them automatically.

Contributions are precise FEC figures. Trades are disclosed as amount ranges and are filed up to 45 days after the trade. Speakers identified by name fallback (rather than C-SPAN's tagging) are marked as lower-confidence.
