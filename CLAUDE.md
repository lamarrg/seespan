# SeeSpan

Chrome MV3 extension that identifies U.S. members of Congress in C-SPAN program videos (via C-SPAN's speaker-tagged transcript API) and overlays their top campaign contributors (FEC) and estimated net worth + recent trades (FMP). See @docs/PROJECT.md for the full PRD.
